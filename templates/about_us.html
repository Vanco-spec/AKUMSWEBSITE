About Us

AKUMS is a vibrant student association dedicated to fostering collaboration, research, and education among aspiring medical professionals. Join us in shaping the future of healthcare through knowledge, innovation, and community outreach.

Core Values

  • Vision: To inspire excellence in medical education and practice.
  • Mission: To create a collaborative platform for medical students to thrive academically and professionally.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards in all our endeavors.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative solutions to healthcare challenges.
  • Community: Building a supportive network for lifelong learning and service.

Kenyatta University Medical Department

The Kenyatta University Medical Department is at the forefront of medical education and research, providing students with the resources and opportunities needed to excel in the healthcare industry. Our esteemed faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure the highest standards of learning and innovation.

Academic Programs

We offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to prepare students for a successful career in medicine. Our programs focus on combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on clinical experience to ensure that our graduates are well-equipped to meet the challenges of modern healthcare.

Research & Innovation

Our department is committed to advancing medical knowledge through cutting-edge research and innovation. We collaborate with leading healthcare institutions and contribute to groundbreaking studies that shape the future of healthcare. Our research programs cover a wide range of medical fields, from basic sciences to clinical and translational research.

Student Support

At Kenyatta University Medical Department, student success is our priority. We offer a variety of support services, including academic counseling, career guidance, and mental health resources. Our dedicated staff ensures that students have the tools and support they need to thrive throughout their studies and beyond.

Our Facilities

Our medical students have access to state-of-the-art laboratories, simulation centers, and clinical spaces. We continually invest in infrastructure to provide our students with the best possible learning environment, ensuring they are well-prepared for their medical careers.

Our Sponsors

We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks and appreciation to our generous sponsors for their unwavering support. Your contributions play a crucial role in helping us achieve our goals, and we are truly grateful for your partnership. We look forward to continuing this journey together!
